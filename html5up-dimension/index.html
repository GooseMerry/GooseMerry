Intro

Welcome to my portfolio. As a creative and strategic project manager, I specialize in transforming visionary concepts into market-ready products within the art, innovation, and creative industries. Explore my projects and discover how I blend artistic vision with business insight to drive growth and sustainability.

I specialize in empowering start-ups to transform their creative ideas into successful ventures. By leveraging my expertise in project management, branding, and digital marketing, I guide start-ups through the critical stages of development. From conceptualization to market launch, I help refine their vision, develop a strong brand identity, and create strategic marketing plans that resonate with their target audience. My hands-on approach ensures that each project is tailored to its unique audience, blending innovative design with business acumen. Whether it’s securing funding, building a digital presence, or navigating the complexities of the start-up landscape, I provide the insights and support needed to drive growth and achieve sustainable success.

Work

Explore my diverse portfolio of projects, including the art gallery launch Art Helica, the music campaign Lullaby Mode, the book start-up Volta Celeste, the art start-up Macondo Dog, and the successful pet product company Cu Pooch. Each project showcases my expertise in project management, branding, and digital marketing, driving engagement and market success. Ongoing Projects in Active Development:

Art Helica (Art Gallery Launch) @arthelica

Stepping into a world where art meets sustainability, Art Helica is an upcoming art gallery set to revolutionize the community engagement scene. With an eclectic mix of immersive installations and workshops, this gallery invites local artists to showcase their work in the most interactive way possible. From eco-friendly materials to zero-waste event planning, every detail is designed with our planet in mind. As the mastermind behind this project, I’ve been shaping its brand identity, forging partnerships with local vendors, and orchestrating a grand opening that promises to be as sustainable as it is spectacular. Stay tuned for the public debut — it’s all under wraps for now!

Lullaby Mode (Music Project) @lullabymode

Enter the realm of Lullaby Mode, where music connects generations. This innovative project bridges established artists with the new wave of fans through experiential campaigns that captivate and engage. My role is to spearhead these groundbreaking campaigns, craft a bespoke product range, and design an interactive e-commerce platform that resonates with both artists and fans alike. Though the details are top-secret for now, rest assured this venture is set to transform the music landscape.

Volta Celeste(Book Start-Up) @voltaceleste

Volta Celeste is more than just a book start-up — it’s a multimedia journey into the heart of the renewable energy movement. Inspired by real-life events, this project weaves a powerful narrative around global sustainability efforts, carbon neutrality, and eco-activism. My responsibilities span managing the e-publishing process, developing podcasts, and scripting a future film adaptation. With a unique marketing approach, we’re leveraging social media and influencer partnerships to inspire an audience passionate about our planet. All specifics are under NDA, but get ready for a launch that will make waves in the sustainability space.

Macondo Dog (Art Start-Up) @macondodog

Inspired by the mystical realism of Gabriel García Márquez’s One Hundred Years of Solitude, Macondo Dog brings a surreal, magical narrative to life through visual art. My journey with this start-up involves crafting the product line, weaving captivating brand stories, and building a strong digital presence. Each piece explores time, memory, and the human experience, deeply rooted in culture and folklore. With a unique digital marketing strategy designed to build anticipation, this project is poised for a grand reveal. Details are under NDA, but prepare for an artistic experience like no other.

Cu Pooch (Dog Products Start-Up) @cupooch

Cu Pooch has successfully carved a niche in the pet product market with its innovative dog walking gear. My role encompassed everything from securing patents and grant funding to establishing the brand’s identity and sourcing all products. I also developed and managed the website and e-commerce platforms, running impactful digital marketing campaigns across social media and Google Ads. This start-up exemplifies how a well-crafted strategy can lead to success in a competitive market.
